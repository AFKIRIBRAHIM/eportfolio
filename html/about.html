About Me

My journey in IT began at the Specialized Institute of Applied Technology, where I studied computer networks and earned a diploma as a Specialized Technician. The program focused mostly on hardware, but ever since I was young, I’ve been more fascinated by software — how websites, apps, and games are built.

That passion led me to continue my studies in Applied Computer Science, and I chose Thomas More University because the ACS program offers a strong, future-oriented foundation, including modern fields like artificial intelligence. I’m very happy with that decision, as I can clearly see my growth both technically and personally.

After earning my bachelor’s degree, I aim to become a web developer, applying the knowledge and skills I gained at Thomas More in a real-world company setting. At the same time, I’m also planning to build my own tech startup.

Outside of tech, I enjoy swimming and horseback riding. One of my more unique hobbies is collecting old-timer cars — something I love doing in my free time.
